Dongsaengs and the Bean Stalk

Travelling down the bean stalk at lightning speed, dongsaeng line try to escape with their riches that they have stolen from the Giant in the sky. From gold coins, golden bongbongie and a golden harp, the great giant Mingyu yells from above angrily! An illustration based on the well known fairytale, Jack and the Bean Stalk!
