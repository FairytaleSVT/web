A Little Place Called Home

Jeonghan is a prince who runs away from the castle, from his home, to live adventures in the forest and in the rest of the kingdoms. A sword hangs from his shoulder, and a nostalgic expression radiates from his face. He does not wear his prince clothes because nobody can know that he comes from royalty, since that will bring him many problems. He is very sorry to leave his home, but at the same time excited about living new adventures.
