Was It Only a Dream?

On one windy night, Seungkwan woke up to a mysterious boy standing on his bedroom window. He reach his hand and held it. He felt his body became light and the boy was holding him tight, leaving his bedroom behind. They flew across the city, into the starlight of the thick sky. He asked where they were going and the boy answered 'to neverland'.
