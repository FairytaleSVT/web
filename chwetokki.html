The Archer

Seungcheol as an archer is inspired by Arjuna, a character from the Mahabarata. While it is originally an epic from India, this piece is based on the Indonesian wayang adaptation. He is potrayed to be a skilled archer who is willing to protect people and someone who is ambitious.
