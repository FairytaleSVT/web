Hidden Cave of the Fae

A curious fae named Soonyoung with his wolf companion wanders around the famous hidden cave known for its celestial waterfall harnessing the power to grant an everlasting bond between two who share a single joint scoop of water from its pool. There he is met with the presence of a resident of the cave named Mingyu and his owl companion and is endeared with his beauty at first sight.
